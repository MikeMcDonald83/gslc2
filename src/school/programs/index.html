Pre-Kindergarten
Pre kindergarten is for children around 3 years old. The emphasis is on playful learning. Typical activities in pre-k includ reading stories, singing songs, and playing independently. Children will strengthen their vocabular, motor skills, and social skills. Pre-k is distinct from VPK, a free statewide program for 4-year-olds. More info on VPK is further down this page.

VPK
Voluntary Pre-Kindergarten is a pre-k program offered to every child in the state of Florida who will enter kindergarten next year. It is designed to build on what children learn in pre-k. There is more structure, and children will engage in deeper learning like numbers, letters, and art. VPK will help new students get used to sitting for longer lengths of time, listening and following the teachers' instructions, and working together with their peers.
